Lentil Salad
An incredibly refreshing salad with a high protein content. Super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • salt
  • 1 1/4 cups lentils
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large roma [plum] tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 small green chili pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 small carrot, shredded
  • 1/2 cup oil-cured black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil
  • 2 hard cooked eggs, cut in half lengthwise

Steps

  1. Bring the salt and water to a boil in a saucepan to high heat. Pour the lentils into the water while stirring constantly. Reduce heat to low, cover, simmer until the lentils are tender but still hold their shape, about 30 mins. Drain well
  2. Transfer the lentils into a mixing bowl and stir in the garlic, seeded tomatoes, green bell pepper, red onion, chile pepper. Add juice of 1 lemon and 1 teaspoon lemon zest. Mix in the cilantro, shredded carrot, olives.
  3. Season with salt and black pepper and drizzle with olive oil; mix well. Refrigerate for at-least an hour to allow the flavors to blend.
  4. Before serving, mix the salad again and add more lemon juice or olive oil if needed. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with sliced eggs
